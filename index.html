FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How much does AEDEXBOOKS cost? +

AEDEXBOOKS is a one-time payment of $149. No monthly subscription, no per-user fees, no recurring charges. Pay once and use it forever — all future updates included. Compare that to $360–$1,000+ per year for QuickBooks.

What do I need to get started? +

Just a Google account. The app runs entirely in your browser — no download, no install. On first sign-in it automatically creates a Google Sheet in your Drive to store all your data.

Where does my data get stored? +

Everything — clients, jobs, invoices, expenses — is stored in a single Google Sheet in your own Google Drive. Aedex Anima LLC never sees, stores, or owns your business data. You can open that sheet directly anytime and share it with your accountant or bookkeeper.

Does it work on my phone? +

Yes — mobile is the primary design target. The app is built to be used on a phone between jobs, not at a desk. You can add it to your home screen like a native app (it's a PWA). Works on iPhone and Android.

I already have clients in a spreadsheet. Do I have to start over? +

No. Because your data lives in a real Google Sheet, you can paste existing clients, jobs, or expense records directly into the sheet. The app reads it on every load. There's no migration tool needed — just copy and paste into the right columns.

Can I send invoices directly to clients? +

Yes. The app generates a professional PDF invoice and emails it to your client directly from your Gmail account with one tap. The email comes from you — not from a third-party service — so it lands in their inbox like a normal email.

How does expense tracking help with taxes? +

Every expense you log is tagged with an IRS Schedule C category — materials, vehicle, subcontractors, tools, phone, insurance, and more. At tax time your records are already organized the way your accountant needs them. You can share the Google Sheet directly with your CPA — no exports or screenshots needed.

Does it work for my trade? I'm not a handyman. +

Yes. AEDEXBOOKS is built for any contractor or service business — plumbers, electricians, roofers, HVAC techs, lawn care, painters, remodelers, general contractors. If you take on jobs and send invoices, it works for you.

What happens to my data if I stop using the app? +

Nothing happens to it — it stays in your Google Drive exactly as-is. You can download it as a CSV or Excel file, open it directly in Google Sheets, or just leave it there. There's no vendor lock-in and nothing to cancel.

How do I get access? +

Click any "Request Access" button on this page and fill out the short form — takes under a minute. We review every request and get you set up usually same day. No sales call, no demo, no credit card.
